Ukwebtools respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1) Account data

If a tool requires login, we store account information (such as email) via our authentication provider (Supabase).

2) Tool usage

We may store limited data needed to provide the service (for example: access status such as trial/paid). We do not sell your personal data.

3) Reviews

If you submit a review, it may be displayed publicly along with the name you provide (or “Anonymous” if you leave it blank). We may remove spam or abusive content.

4) Cookies

Our tools may use essential cookies/local storage for login sessions and basic functionality.

5) Your choices

You can request deletion of your account data by emailing us. Some records may be retained if required for security or fraud prevention.

Privacy questions: support@ukwebtools.com
