Privacy Policy

Last updated:

ProfitPilot is an independent tool by Ukwebtools. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Account email (for login). Authentication is managed via Supabase.
  • Access data needed to enforce trial/PRO status (e.g., trial start time, feature usage counters).
  • Reviews you submit (name optional, rating, message). Reviews appear publicly.

What we don’t collect

  • We do not collect card/payment details (checkout is handled by Gumroad).
  • We do not connect to your marketplace accounts.

How we use information

  • To provide login, trial access, and PRO unlocking.
  • To prevent abuse and keep the product stable.
  • To display reviews publicly when submitted.

Third parties

  • Supabase for authentication and database storage.
  • Gumroad for payments and license delivery.

Account deletion

You can request deletion by emailing support@ukwebtools.com.
